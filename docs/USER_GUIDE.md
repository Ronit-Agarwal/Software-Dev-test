## ASL Translation Mode

ASL Translation mode uses AI to recognize American Sign Language signs in real-time.

### How to Use

1. **Start Translation Mode**
   - Tap the ASL Translation button on the home screen
   - Or select "Translation" from the mode toggle

2. **Position Your Hands**
   - Place your hands clearly in view of the camera
   - Ensure good lighting on your hands
   - Keep hands steady and well-framed

3. **Sign Clearly**
   - Use standard ASL hand shapes and movements
   - Make signs distinctly and completely
   - Allow 1-2 seconds between different signs

### Features

#### Static Sign Recognition
- **Alphabet** - A through Z fingerspelling
- **Common Words** - Basic vocabulary (hello, goodbye, please, thank you)
- **Numbers** - 0 through 9
- **Real-time Processing** - Instant recognition as you sign

#### Dynamic Sign Recognition
- **Multi-sign Sequences** - Recognition of sign phrases
- **Temporal Analysis** - Understands sign combinations
- **Context Awareness** - Uses surrounding signs for accuracy

#### Translation Display
- **Text Output** - Recognized signs converted to text
- **Confidence Levels** - Shows recognition confidence
- **Translation History** - Save and review past translations

### Tips for Best Results

#### Lighting
- Use bright, even lighting
- Avoid backlighting (lights behind you)
- Natural daylight works best
- Avoid harsh shadows on hands

#### Hand Positioning
- Keep hands 12-18 inches from camera
- Center hands in the frame
- Avoid covering your face with hands
- Keep background simple and uncluttered

#### Signing Technique
- Use clear, deliberate movements
- Hold final hand position for 1 second
- Make signs at chest level when possible
- Use facial expressions as appropriate in ASL

#### Troubleshooting
- **No Recognition**: Check lighting and hand positioning
- **Wrong Letters**: Ensure clear finger spelling
- **Slow Response**: Allow more time between signs
- **Low Confidence**: Try signing more deliberately

## Object Detection Mode

Object Detection mode identifies objects in your surroundings using the camera.

### How to Use

1. **Enter Detection Mode**
   - Tap "Object Detection" from the main menu
   - Or switch using the mode toggle

2. **Point Camera**
   - Aim camera at objects you want to identify
   - Keep camera steady for best results
   - Objects should be clearly visible

3. **View Results**
   - Detected objects are highlighted with bounding boxes
   - Labels show object names and confidence levels
   - Spatial audio alerts for important objects

### Supported Objects

#### Common Objects
- **People** - Adults, children, groups
- **Vehicles** - Cars, trucks, buses, bicycles
- **Furniture** - Chairs, tables, desks, sofas
- **Electronics** - Phones, computers, TVs, lamps
- **Food Items** - Various foods and beverages

#### Accessibility-Focused Objects
- **Doors and Windows** - Important for navigation
- **Stairs and Ramps** - Accessibility navigation
- **Signs and Labels** - Text and symbols
- **Emergency Equipment** - Fire extinguishers, first aid

### Audio Alerts

The app can speak detected objects aloud:

- **Spatial Audio** - "Person at 3 o'clock"
- **Distance Estimates** - "Chair approximately 6 feet ahead"
- **Priority Alerts** - Important objects get higher priority
- **Customizable Sensitivity** - Adjust alert frequency

### Customization

#### Detection Settings
- **Confidence Threshold** - Set minimum confidence for alerts
- **Alert Frequency** - How often to announce objects
- **Object Categories** - Enable/disable specific object types
- **Audio Preferences** - Turn spatial audio on/off

#### Visual Settings
- **Bounding Box Colors** - Choose detection box colors
- **Text Size** - Adjust label font size
- **High Contrast Mode** - Enhanced visibility for low vision

## Face Recognition

Face Recognition allows you to enroll faces for person identification.

### How to Use

1. **Enroll Your Face**
   - Go to Settings → Face Recognition
   - Tap "Enroll New Person"
   - Follow the enrollment process

2. **Enrollment Process**
   - Look directly at the camera
   - Move your head slightly as prompted
   - Ensure good lighting
   - Complete all required poses

3. **Recognition**
   - Once enrolled, recognized faces are identified
   - See names displayed on screen
   - Get audio notifications for recognized people

### Privacy and Security

- Face data is stored locally on your device
- No face data is sent to external servers
- You can delete enrolled faces anytime
- Recognition works offline

### Tips for Enrollment

#### Lighting
- Use bright, even lighting
- Avoid shadows on your face
- Natural lighting works best
- Avoid direct overhead lights

#### Positioning
- Look directly at the camera
- Keep face centered in frame
- Maintain comfortable distance
- Remove glasses or hats if possible

#### Quality
- Keep face still during scans
- Follow all pose prompts
- Ensure face is clearly visible
- Avoid motion blur

## Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions for SignSync.

### Camera Issues

#### Camera Not Working

**Symptoms:**
- Black screen in camera view
- "Camera unavailable" error message
- Camera fails to initialize

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Permissions**
   - Go to Settings → Apps → SignSync → Permissions
   - Ensure Camera permission is granted
   - Restart the app after granting permission

2. **Close Other Apps**
   - Close other camera apps (Photo, Video)
   - Restart SignSync
   - Try again

3. **Restart Device**
   - Close all apps completely
   - Restart your phone
   - Launch SignSync again

4. **Check Camera Settings**
   - Try different camera resolution
   - Switch between front/back camera
   - Disable flash if causing issues

#### Poor Image Quality

**Symptoms:**
- Blurry or pixelated images
- Low lighting issues
- Slow recognition

**Solutions:**
1. **Improve Lighting**
   - Move to better lit area
   - Use natural light when possible
   - Avoid backlighting

2. **Adjust Settings**
   - Increase camera resolution
   - Enable auto focus
   - Adjust exposure if available

3. **Clean Camera Lens**
   - Gently clean camera lens
   - Remove any dirt or smudges
   - Use microfiber cloth if needed

### ASL Detection Issues

#### Signs Not Recognized

**Symptoms:**
- No text appears when signing
- Incorrect letters or words
- Very low confidence scores

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Hand Positioning**
   - Keep hands 12-18 inches from camera
   - Center hands in frame
   - Avoid covering face

2. **Improve Signing**
   - Sign more deliberately and slowly
   - Hold final position for 1 second
   - Use standard ASL hand shapes

3. **Adjust Settings**
   - Lower confidence threshold
   - Increase temporal smoothing
   - Check lighting conditions

4. **Update Models**
   - Ensure latest ML models are loaded
   - Restart app to reload models
   - Check for app updates

#### Slow Recognition

**Symptoms:**
- Long delay between signing and text
- Laggy camera feed
- High battery usage

**Solutions:**
1. **Optimize Performance**
   - Lower camera resolution
   - Reduce frame rate to 15 FPS
   - Close other apps

2. **Check Device Resources**
   - Restart device
   - Clear app cache
   - Ensure sufficient storage space

3. **Update App**
   - Install latest app version
   - Update ML models
   - Check device compatibility

### Sound Detection Issues

#### No Sound Alerts

**Symptoms:**
- No notifications for loud sounds
- Microphone not responding
- "Microphone access denied"

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Microphone Permission**
   - Go to Settings → Apps → SignSync → Permissions
   - Ensure Microphone is granted
   - Restart app after granting

2. **Adjust Sensitivity**
   - Lower noise threshold
   - Test with loud sounds
   - Check audio settings

3. **Device Settings**
   - Check device volume settings
   - Ensure device microphone works
   - Try other audio apps

#### Too Many False Alerts

**Symptoms:**
- Frequent alerts for normal sounds
- Irritating alert frequency
- Battery drain from constant monitoring

**Solutions:**
1. **Adjust Sensitivity**
   - Increase noise threshold
   - Disable less important alert types
   - Use specific sound categories

2. **Customize Alerts**
   - Turn off haptic feedback
   - Reduce visual alerts
   - Set quiet hours

### Face Recognition Issues

#### Enrollment Failed

**Symptoms:**
- "Face enrollment failed" message
- Poor recognition accuracy
- Repeated enrollment prompts

**Solutions:**
1. **Improve Conditions**
   - Use better lighting
   - Keep face still during scan
   - Remove glasses or hats

2. **Multiple Attempts**
   - Try enrollment multiple times
   - Vary head positions slightly
   - Ensure face is clearly visible

3. **Device Compatibility**
   - Check if device supports face recognition
   - Update camera app
   - Restart device and retry

#### Recognition Not Working

**Symptoms:**
- Unknown faces show as recognized
- No recognition alerts
- Inconsistent identification

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Enrollment Quality**
   - Re-enroll faces with better quality
   - Ensure sufficient lighting
   - Use multiple poses

2. **Update Recognition**
   - Refresh face database
   - Re-train recognition model
   - Clear and re-enroll if needed

### Performance Issues

#### App Running Slowly

**Symptoms:**
- Slow app startup
- Laggy interface
- Delayed responses

**Solutions:**
1. **Device Optimization**
   - Close other running apps
   - Restart device
   - Free up storage space

2. **App Settings**
   - Reduce camera quality
   - Lower frame rate
   - Disable unused features

3. **Updates**
   - Update to latest app version
   - Update device OS
   - Clear app cache

#### High Battery Usage

**Symptoms:**
- Rapid battery drain
- Device heating up
- Short usage time

**Solutions:**
1. **Power Settings**
   - Reduce camera resolution
   - Lower frame rate to 15 FPS
   - Disable always-on features

2. **Background Usage**
   - Limit background processing
   - Turn off notifications when not needed
   - Use power saving mode

### Connectivity Issues

#### Internet Connection Problems

**Symptoms:**
- AI assistant not working
- Model downloads failing
- Sync issues

**Solutions:**
1. **Network Check**
   - Test internet connection
   - Try different WiFi network
   - Use cellular data temporarily

2. **Network Settings**
   - Check firewall settings
   - Verify DNS settings
   - Contact ISP if issues persist
